16-Week Growth Journey

This class is a 16-week basic growth course. It is designed to be automatic yet requires your absolute commitment. The timetable will guide you on days and times when you will be called upon for a review of your specific topic.

If you've gotten this far, it means you're practically interested in Growth.

  • 02 Attend all sessions consistently.
  • 03 Participate actively in discussions.

Disqualifying Behaviors

The following will lead to immediate removal from the academy:

  • Missing classes repeatedly without notice.
  • Consistently joining late or leaving early.
  • Failing to complete assignments or reviews.
  • Disrespecting facilitators or peers.
  • Sharing training materials without permission.
  • Ignoring or breaking class instructions.
  • Cheating or the unauthorized use of AI.
  • Acting in ways that dishonor the Spirit of the training.

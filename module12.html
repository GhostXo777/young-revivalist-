Module 12

The Blessings of Abraham

Heirs According to the Promise

Galatians 3:14 tells us that the blessing of Abraham comes on the Gentiles through Christ Jesus. In this session, we study the nature of the Abrahamic covenant—a covenant of faith, prosperity, and multiplication—and how we, as New Testament believers, are the legal heirs to every promise made to him.

The Power to Get Wealth
